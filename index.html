Why VeriPharm?

Medical errors related to medications—such as incorrect drug combinations, lack of systemic interaction control, and fragmented patient data—pose a critical risk to patient safety.

VeriPharm solves this by providing a centralized system for analyzing, prescribing, and tracking medications, acting as a reliable digital assistant for doctors and pharmacists.

Core Features

Medication Management

Comprehensive storage of drug data, intuitive therapy assignment, and continuous medication tracking.

Drug Interaction Analysis

Automated compatibility checks to identify potential risks and prevent dangerous drug combinations.

Barcode Scanning

Fast and accurate medication identification via integrated mobile barcode scanning.

PDF Reports

Generate professional, structured medical reports for doctors, pharmacists, and patients.
